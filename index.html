F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of junk do you remove?

We remove furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and general household or commercial junk. If you're unsure about an item, just ask — chances are we can take it.

How does your pricing work?

Pricing is based on how much space your items take up in our truck. You’ll receive a clear, upfront quote before we start — no hidden fees or surprise charges.

Do I need to move the junk outside first?

Not at all. Our team does all the lifting, hauling, and cleanup. Just point to what you want removed and we’ll take care of the rest.

What happens to the junk after pickup?

We aim to recycle, repurpose, or donate items whenever possible. Only items that cannot be reused are responsibly disposed of at approved facilities.

Do you offer same-day or next-day service?

Yes! Same-day and next-day pickups are often available depending on location and availability. Contact us early and we’ll do our best to schedule you quickly.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Our team is fully licensed and insured so you can feel confident knowing your property and belongings are protected.
